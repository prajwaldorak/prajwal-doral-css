Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 8 slices of bread
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 8 slices of bacon
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • Butter for spreading

Instructions

1. Heat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the unbuttered side of 4 slices of bread.

4. Add 2 slices of bacon and tomato slices on top of the cheese.

5. Top with another slice of cheese and a slice of bread, buttered side facing up.

6. Place sandwiches on the skillet or griddle and cook until golden brown on both sides and cheese is melted, about 3-4 minutes per side.

7. Remove from heat and serve immediately.
